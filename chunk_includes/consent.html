Purpose of the study

In this study, Adina Camelia Bleotu from ZAS Berlin and University of Bucharest and Anton Benz from ZAS Berlin are investigating some linguistic phenomena in English. The information collected in this study will be used to prepare papers for linguistic conferences and journals. You are eligible to take part in this study because you are a native English speaker older than 18 years old.

Procedures

In this study, you will have to read some sentences, and then you will have to decide whether you can conclude from those sentences that other sentences hold. The whole process should last around 15-20 minutes. Please make sure that you have answered all the questions carefully. After completing the test, you will receive a code which you can enter on Prolific.

Risks

There are no risks in this experimental procedure. However, in online research, there is always a risk of a breach of confidentiality. In order to prevent this from happening, we will not be collecting information that uniquely identifies participants on the servers where our data is collected.

Benefits

Data confidentiality

Your answers will remain confidential. Your name will not appear in any paper or conference presentation. In order to ensure confidentiality, your answers will be associated with a number even within our own analyses.

For more information

If you have any questions about the study, you can send an e-mail to cameliableotu@gmail.com

Refusal to take part in or retreating from the study

Your participation in this study is purely voluntary. You do not have to provide information against your will. You can choose to give up at any point, but, in that case, you will not receive a reward.

Terms and conditions

By taking part in this experiment, I confirm that I am at least 18 years old and that I understand the purpose of the research, the procedures that I will subject myself to, the possible risks and the possible benefits. Therefore, I consent to take part as a subject in this research project.
